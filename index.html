About

I'm a Policy Researcher at Apollo Research, where I've worked on reports on AI incident regimes, the internal deployment of AI systems, and a literature review of the field of mechanistic intepretability. Before that I did independent AI governance research, on anti-tamper self-destruct mechanisms for GPUs, voluntary safety in nuclear power and aviation, and the history of the Nuclear Regulatory Commission.

In a past life, I was an avid quizzer and student of Physics and Philosophy at Bristol.
